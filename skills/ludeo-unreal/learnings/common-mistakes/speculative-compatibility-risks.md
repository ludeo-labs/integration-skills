Do not list speculative engine version compatibility as a risk unless there is concrete evidence of incompatibility. The LudeoUESDK plugin states support for "UE 4.26 or later" — flagging a specific UE version as a risk without evidence is invention, not analysis. Only flag compatibility risks when you have found actual API conflicts, deprecation warnings, or confirmed reports.
